Decade Shooter = Lifelong Improvement

Lifelong improvement in your golf game starts with setting meaningful, high-quality goals. Many golfers set goals that sound great but are often unrealistic:

• "I am going to be a scratch golfer!"

• "I am going to shoot my age!"

The reality is that unless you are in the top 1 - 3% of golfers, these goals may be out of reach.

Instead, challenge yourself with a goal that is both ambitious and achievable - become a Decade Shooter! Aim to shoot your decade and track your progress over time.

Join us and make every round a step toward success!

Who is a Decade Shooter?

A Decade Shooter is a golfer who can shoot their decade. Golfers ages 70-79 can break 80, golfers ages 80-89 can break 90, etc.

A Decade Shooter is golfer that sets smart goals in order to achieve Decade Shooter status.

Snapshot of a Decade Shooter

Golf is not just a pastime - it's a passion!

• Decade Shooters may regularly go to a PGA Superstore or local pro-shop just to browse, and walk out with a new driver.

• Decade Shooters regularly monitors their handicap index on GHIN or other resource.

• A Decade Shooter is usually 65 and over. Golfers ages 65-69 are working towards become a Decade Shooter in their 70's.

• Decade Shooters may have a cache of saved golf videos to watch while waiting for the chiropractor.
